In Memory – Pastor Doris

Pastor Doris J. Swartz ( November 27, 1933 – May 22, 2012 )

We have all been very richly blessed by Pastor Doris and will certainly feel the loss of her kind, Godly and seasoned counsel, practical application of God’s Word, endless testimonies of her travels across the globe and, of course, her voluminous and consistent love for each of us. Her legacy lives on through us and the countless lives of those she has touched throughout the uttermost parts of this earth.

As we realize the void left in each of our lives, we more so rejoice that she is now experiencing the unhindered Presence of God she so desperately desired each of us to grab a hold of in this present life.

We must all determine to live a life of integrity, be hungery for God and thirst for souls. By doing this, we will adequately honor her life and her dedication to live the Godly life she lived.

Thank you Lord for the gift of Pastor Doris.
